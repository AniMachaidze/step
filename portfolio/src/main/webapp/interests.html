My Interests

Photography

I have been interested in Photography for a long time, however last year was the first time when I got formal education in Photography. I took a course at Penn called FNAR 340 Introduction to digital photography. I learned a lot about different types of photography, cameras and tools used to edit pictures, as well as understanding and critiquing pictures. I enjoy portrait photography, depicting people in their natural positions and actions.
